# Welcome to Jellyfin Elevate

Jellyfin Elevate is a single plugin that makes Jellyfin 12 feel finished. It sharpens the parts of the app you touch every day — the player, the library, search — and adds the things power users always end up wanting: streaming availability, a trending feed, one-click requests through Seerr, Sonarr and Radarr integration, spoiler protection, and branding that makes the server unmistakably yours.

It's an independent fork of [Jellyfin Enhanced](about.md), rebuilt from the ground up for Jellyfin 12. Nothing here is vaporware — every feature on this site ships in the plugin, documented exactly as it behaves.

## New here? Start with setup

Installing takes a few minutes: add the repository, install from the catalog, restart, and the Enhanced Panel appears. From there, a short first-run checklist points you at the handful of things worth turning on.

[Get started →](getting-started.md)

## What's inside

- **[The Enhanced Experience](enhanced.md)** — keyboard shortcuts, custom subtitles, a pause screen, bookmarks, poster tags, and per-user hidden content. The day-to-day polish.
- **[Discover & Request](discover.md)** — see where a title is streaming, browse a trending feed inside your library, and request what you don't have through Seerr.
- **[Sonarr & Radarr](sonarr-radarr.md)** — jump from an item straight into your \*arr apps, run interactive searches, and sync tags. Admin-only.
- **[Spoiler Guard](spoiler-guard.md)** — blur artwork and hide revealing metadata for anything you haven't watched yet.
- **[Customization](customization.md)** — brand the server, pick a theme, and reach the admin controls for maintenance mode, layout, and caches.
- **[Reference](reference.md)** and the **[Developer Guide](developers.md)** — where every setting lives, the custom-CSS hooks, and the REST API.
